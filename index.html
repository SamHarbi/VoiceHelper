Voice Helper

About

Voice Helper is an experimental Hackathon Submission that aims to increase accessibility to technology among those who have problems using traditional input methods. In the case of this prototype, it is Keyboards for typing tasks.

Note: Speech Recognition only works with Chrome Browsers and likely other Chromium based Browsers- This also includes Android Devices.
